Programme Details

Things to look forward to!

Sharings by NGO Founders

Participants will gain unique insights on starting a ground-up organisation, from individuals who have done so themselves.

Sharings by NGO Founders

Participants will gain unique insights on starting a ground-up organisation, from individuals who have done so themselves.

Topical Discussions on Societal Issues

Participants will learn more about existing efforts by social organisations and government agencies relating to specific areas of interest, including poverty, the environment, differently-abled individuals and migrant workers.

Skills Upgrading

Participants will be equipped with best practices necessary to start sustainable service learning projects, as told through the journeys of various prominent Service Learning Projects.

Skills Upgrading

Focus Group Discussions

Based on their passions, participants will be grouped into interest groups, and will deepen their understanding in each societal issue and propose workable community initiatives, while building new friendships.

Dialogue on a Career in Social Service

Participants will have a deeper understanding of what it means to serve in the social service sector, from those who are in this meaningful sector themselves. Participants will also learn about possible career pathways and scholarship opportunities in this sector.
