About this year's theme

“Service”. The word that started it all.

Service underpins whatever we do, in Hwa Chong Interact. Service is an experience, as we share our stories with our beneficiaries and grow from them. Service is a journey, one that enables us to soar to greater heights even as we become more grounded. Service is a spirit that drives us to do even more for the community, motivating us to start new events that make an impact.

Most importantly, to instill a purpose of "Service" in all our participants is our very goal for the Service Learning Summit. So what does "Service" mean to you? Let’s find out!

About Hwa Chong Interact

36 years in the making

Hwa Chong Interact was founded in 1985. As one of the largest and most popular CCAs in Hwa Chong Institution, we volunteer with six different beneficiaries on a weekly basis.

We've also embarked on various local and international service projects such as the Asia Clean Project focused on menstrual hygiene, Project Bisrama which seeks to improve the welfare of Foreign Domestic Workers (FDWs) and the Public Health Ambassadors project which seeks to support our healthcare system during the COVID-19 pandemic.
