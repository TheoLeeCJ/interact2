Guest-of-Honour

Mr David Chua

Chief Executive Officer, National Youth Council

David was appointed Chief Executive Officer of the National Youth Council in July 2015, becoming the autonomous agency’s first CEO. NYC was restructured into an autonomous agency under the Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth in January 2015, and operates as the national body coordinating holistic youth development. It also houses Outward Bound Singapore and Youth Corps Singapore. David is also concurrently the Chairman of *SCAPE.

As the CEO of NYC, his responsibilities include expanding developmental opportunities and aspirational pathways for young Singaporeans, while ensuring equal and fair access to these networks.
