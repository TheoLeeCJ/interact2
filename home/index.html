Welcome

About SLS

Our Vision

To develop a stronger youth volunteerism ecosystem, to encourage youths to build a more inclusive and caring Singapore.

Our Aims

1. Community

To provide a platform for students from different schools interested in serving the community to share best practices and co-create new community-based ideas

2. Leadership

To equip students with the necessary skills to be effective leaders in service learning and the community

3. Growth

To help students learn more about the true meaning of service.

Message from Chairperson of SLS

Dear All,

The Service Learning Summit was started to address a simple question - "What does service mean to you?" Many of us and our friends had volunteered in various capacities, but almost no one had a definite answer to this.

In 2018, President Halimah Yacob shared that "more than one in three youths volunteer, up from one in five in 2014". More youths are realising the importance of reaching out to the community, and are making an impact. However, some may not understand the true meaning of service, and may not be equipped with skills to serve their beneficiaries meaningfully.

To a CCA which volunteers with six different organisations every week and whose mission is "Service Above Self", learning more about the meaning of service is an existential question. This led us to embark on this journey which has led us to organise the first-ever Service Learning Summit, after 36 years on the ground.

From the very start, we were determined to make the Service Learning Summit a reality. The Service Learning Summit will be a multi-faceted platform for student leaders across Singapore schools with a strong interest in service learning. Our participants will upscale their volunteering programmes and reach out to the community with a renewed sense of purpose. This event will also bring together students from other schools with a burning passion to serve.
We hope to see you!

Sincerely,
Koh Heer Lin
Chair, Service Learning Summit

About our logo

As a new event, we’ve thought about what would best represent the spirit underpinning the Service Learning Summit, and this is the Carepybara! Capybaras are highly sociable, and are usually found in large groups of friends and family. More than that, they care for one another!
